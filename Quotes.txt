====== Quotes ======
Created Mittwoch 04 Oktober 2023

//The person who achieves his goal is the one that has faced frustration and dealt with it, understood it, and used it to their advantage … We repeatedly fell, were knocked down, ignored. And we kept getting up, and we didn’t give up.//
In a speech to a class of freshly graduated physicians in 2021, Nobel prizewinner Drew Weissman wished them a lifetime of frustration.


//You can have a scientific impact, but you can also have an impact by being a good mentor or giving somebody the confidence they need to do a hard thing//
Jenny Hoffman, Interniew with nature, https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-024-00369-1


//Simply preposterous.//
Anthony Fauci, the face of the US pandemic response during both the Trump and Biden administrations, dismissed allegations at a hearing of US lawmakers that his agency funded research that created the COVID-19 pandemic or that he coordinated a cover-up of the pandemic’s origins, https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-024-01657-6


//Knowledge is like a shark: it must keep moving to stay alive.//
César Hidalgo, article in Financial Times "How economies forget", https://www.ft.com/content/fba0f841-5bfe-49b5-b686-6bc7732837bb


//Pour ce qui est de l'avenir, il ne s'agit pas de le prévoir, mais de le rendre possible. (As for the future, your task is not to foresee it, but to enable it.)//
Antoine de Saint-Exupéry, Citadelle (1948)


//For a successful technology, reality must take precedence over public relations, for Nature cannot be fooled.//
Richard Feynman, report on the shuttle Challenger accident in 1986


//Behandle die Menschen so, als wären sie, was sie sein sollten, und du hilfst ihnen zu werden, was sie sein können. (Treat people as if they were what they ought to be and you help them to become what they are capable of being)//
Johann Wolfgang von Goethe, original quote seems to be "Wenn wir sagtest Du, die Menschen nur nehmen, wie sie sind, so machen wir sie schlechter; wenn wir sie behandeln als wären sie, was sie sein sollten, so bringen wir sie dahin, wohin sie zu bringen sind." from Wilhem Meisters Lehrjahre (1795/96)


//Reading maketh a full man; conference a ready man; and writing an exact man.//
Sir Francis Bacon, essay “Of Studies” (1597)


//The faintest ink is better than the strongest memory.//
Chinese proverb


//Funding = Fusion. It turns out the most important skillset for fusion science is not actually plasma physics after all. It is the ability to raise the finance.//
Richard Dinan, The Fusion Age (2017), in general 2/5
