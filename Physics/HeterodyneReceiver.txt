====== HeterodyneReceiver ======
Created Donnerstag 14 Mai 2020

* incoming signal frequency is mixed to a lower intermediate frequency
* RF signal processing and filtering happens at that intermediate frequency
* in general, heterodyning describe the process of combining or mixing two frequencies and thus creating a new frequency
