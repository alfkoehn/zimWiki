====== Zotero ======
Created Dienstag 13 September 2022

Zotero type **Presentation**:
* talk at a conference
* poster at a conference
* //Meeting Name// is for the name of the conference

Zotero type **Conference paper**:
* for published paper in a conference proceedings (not just a presentation)
* //Proceedings Title// will be used by most styles, should contain "Proceedings of that nice conference"
* //Conference Name// will be used only by small number of styles
